## Purpose & Mission

The GEISEL ANKI Generator supports medical education by:
- Facilitating efficient flashcard creation from lecture materials
- Leveraging AI to generate high-quality study materials
- Supporting USMLE preparation
- Making evidence-based learning tools accessible to medical students

## Target Audience

- Geisel medical students
- Medical students preparing for USMLE Step 1
- Medical educators creating study materials
- Anyone studying medical sciences

## Features Aligned with Educational Research

The app incorporates evidence-based learning principles:
- **Active recall:** Flashcard-based learning
- **Spaced repetition:** ANKI's SRS algorithm
- **AI-enhanced learning:** Claude AI for content generation
- **Metacognition:** Review and selection process
